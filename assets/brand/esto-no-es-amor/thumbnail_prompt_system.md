# Sistema De Thumbnails: Esto No Es Amor

## Prompt Base

Create a premium editorial YouTube thumbnail background for the relationship-healing podcast channel "Esto no es amor".

Visual identity: intimate editorial photography, deep charcoal background, warm amber practical light, deep teal shadows, cinematic contrast, tactile objects, quiet emotional tension, magazine cover quality, premium Spanish podcast aesthetic.

Use object-led emotional metaphors only: empty chair, lamp, key, mirror, notebook, cup, phone face down, red thread, open door, folded letter, table after a conversation.

No faces, no hands, no fingers, no people as subject, no microphones, no speakers, no headphones, no readable text, no logos, no clinical therapy office, no cold hospital aesthetic.

Composition must leave clean negative space for local title overlay. The title text will be added later by software, so the generated image must be text-free.

## Variacion Por Video

Change at least three of these per video:

- object;
- camera angle;
- room layout;
- light source;
- foreground texture;
- emotional metaphor;
- color balance between amber and teal.

## Overlay Local

Always place the exact video title locally after image generation. Never ask the image model to render Spanish title text.
